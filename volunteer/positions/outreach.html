OUTREACH

Outreach and Web Development team members are responsible for posting on social media to promote Crafts for Charity and updating our website as necessary. Tasks may differ on a week by week basis and will be assigned during weekly online meetings.

Time Commitment

Each week, Crafts for Charity Outreach and Web Development members are expected to attend one team call. The calls will last between 30 minutes to 1 hour. Tasks will be assigned during these calls and weekly tasks will take between 30 minutes to 3 hours. CFC operates on a season by season basis and members are expected to stay through full seasons. The current Winter season lasts from December 11th through March 5th.

Benefits

  • Help your community and local charities
  • Earn service hours for all work completed for Crafts for Charity
  • Build stronger communication, creativity, and marketing skills as well as gain experience working with teams within an organization
  • Gain experience working on websites
  • Gain valuable experience that can be used on resumes for future jobs, internships, or college applications

Requirements

  • Aged 14+
    • Exceptions may be made to highly qualified 13 year olds
  • Strong communication skills
  • Ability to work on Crafts for Charity tasks for at least 2 hours a week

Desirable Qualifications

  • Previous volunteer experience
  • Art and graphic design experience
  • Experience with video editing
  • Strong writing experience
  • Website coding experience (HTML & CSS)
