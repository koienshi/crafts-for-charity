VIRTUAL CLASSES

Our knitting and crocheting classes are meant for 1-3 students, and they are led by two teachers. They are usually around 45-90 minutes long, and it will take about 2 more hours outside of the class to finish a rectangle on your own time. Lessons are generally held on Google Meet, but feel free to request a different call platform. You will receive more information about this when you sign up for a class! (You can sign up on the Register for A Class page) Once you are done knitting your rectangle, you can give it to us so we can donate your craft to a charity! There are two options for doing this, one is for you to hand it to a CFC volunteer (we will give you an address if you choose this option) or you can drop it off at your nearest drop-off box location! You can find a drop-off box location on the Drop-Off Crafts or Materials page! Once we receive your rectangle(s), we will use it to create a scarf or blanket with other rectangles and donate it to a charity in need!
